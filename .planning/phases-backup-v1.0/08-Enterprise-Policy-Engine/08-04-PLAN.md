<objective>
Implement audit, metrics, and evidence generation for the Enterprise Policy Engine. This plan delivers: (1) DecisionAuditPublisher emitting structured policy audit events (policy_decision_recorded, rate_limit_exceeded, spend_limit_exceeded, routing_policy_violation, classification_block); (2) Prometheus counters (anonreq_policy_decisions_total, anonreq_policy_denials_total, anonreq_rate_limit_hits_total, anonreq_spend_limit_hits_total); (3) Evidence store with SHA-256 hash manifests; (4) budget_reset audit event at UTC boundaries; and (5) All audit/metadata-only invariants enforced.

Purpose: Without audit and metrics, operators cannot observe policy enforcement behavior, quantify tenant usage, or provide compliance evidence during regulatory review.
</objective>
