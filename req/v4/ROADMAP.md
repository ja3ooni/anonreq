# AnonReq v4 Roadmap

## Vision

Expand AnonReq from a secure AI gateway into an enterprise AI trust, compliance, and governed productivity platform.

## Guiding Principles

- Keep the fail-secure foundation intact.
- Treat governance, compliance evidence, and policy enforcement as first-class product capabilities.
- Expand into governed productivity workflows without compromising data sovereignty.
- Prioritize enterprise deployment readiness, auditability, and operational control.

## Phase 1 — Trust & Compliance Foundation

### Goal
Establish the core V4 control plane for governance, evidence, and policy administration.

### Scope
- governance workspace and owner model
- approval workflow for policy and configuration changes
- compliance evidence collection and export
- audit and configuration history improvements
- trust center MVP
- vendor inventory and third-party risk basics

### Outcome
AnonReq becomes a credible trust and compliance platform for enterprise buyers.

## Phase 2 — Secure Productivity & Workflow Expansion

### Goal
Extend the platform into governed AI productivity use cases.

### Scope
- multimodal document intake and anonymization
- secure document review and summarization
- meeting and voice protection workflows
- governed coding assistant and drafting workflows
- internal knowledge Q&A with policy-aware retrieval
- multilingual drafting and translation workflows

### Outcome
Customers can use AnonReq for high-value regulated productivity workflows without exposing sensitive data.

## Phase 3 — Agent & Enterprise Platform Scale

### Goal
Turn AnonReq into a full enterprise AI governance platform.

### Scope
- agent and tool governance
- human approval gates for sensitive actions
- shadow AI and CASB-style visibility
- broader SIEM, ticketing, and collaboration integrations
- advanced admin portal and operational dashboards
- large-scale policy orchestration and evidence reporting

### Outcome
AnonReq supports enterprise-wide AI governance, enforcement, and assurance across users, tools, agents, and workflows.

## Success Criteria

By the end of V4, AnonReq should be able to:
- provide continuous trust and compliance evidence
- govern prompts, documents, agents, and workflows
- support secure productivity use cases inside the enterprise perimeter
- integrate into enterprise security, compliance, and operations workflows
