# AnonReq v4 Feature Set

## Purpose

This document defines the proposed V4 product feature set for AnonReq, based on the current requirements foundation and the product capabilities represented by Vanta and NodeShift. The goal is to expand AnonReq from a secure AI gateway into a broader enterprise AI trust, compliance, and productivity platform.

## V4 Product Positioning

AnonReq v4 is positioned as:

- a self-hosted AI trust and governance platform for regulated enterprises
- an enforcement layer for AI data protection, compliance automation, and workflow governance
- a platform that combines secure AI access, compliance evidence, and governed productivity experiences

## V4 Feature Themes

### 1. AI Trust & Governance Control Plane

Features:
- tenant-level governance workspace
- named AI governance owners and review workflow
- risk register and treatment tracking
- approval workflow for production policy changes
- governance dashboard and executive reporting
- evidence export for audits and regulatory review

### 2. Continuous Compliance Automation

Features:
- automated evidence collection for SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, HITRUST, and other frameworks
- control monitoring and drift detection
- questionnaire automation and draft response generation
- remediation task tracking and ownership
- compliance scorecards and progress reporting

### 3. Vendor & Third-Party Risk Management

Features:
- AI vendor inventory and supplier governance
- third-party risk scoring and review workflow
- contract status and renewal tracking
- suspended-provider enforcement and alerting
- security review and due-diligence evidence repository

### 4. Trust Center & Customer Assurance

Features:
- public or customer-facing trust center
- live compliance posture and evidence visibility
- customer questionnaire response portal
- control statement publishing
- security posture snapshots and attestation sharing

### 5. Policy Engine & AI Firewall Expansion

Features:
- policy-driven classification and enforcement for prompts, outputs, tools, and agents
- prompt injection and jailbreak detection
- outbound and inbound AI security controls
- output safety and policy violation review
- policy simulation and testing framework

### 6. Admin & Operations Workspace

Features:
- centralized admin portal for tenants, policies, providers, and evidence
- role-based access control for administrators, security officers, operators, and auditors
- configuration change history and immutable audit trail
- operational dashboards for SLOs, incidents, and policy events
- incident response workflow and case management

### 7. Multimodal & Document Intelligence

Features:
- document intake and anonymization for contracts, reports, and policy documents
- OCR and document parsing support
- cross-document retrieval and Q&A with governance controls
- secure document review and summarization workflows
- structured extraction and clause analysis for regulated content

### 8. Meeting, Voice, and Collaboration Protection

Features:
- transcription and summarization protection for meetings and calls
- voice and conversation anonymization workflows
- meeting-note and action-item generation under governance controls
- secure collaboration assistants for email, chat, and knowledge work

### 9. Agent & Tool Governance

Features:
- inspection of agent tool calls and MCP-style interactions
- per-agent and per-tool permission policies
- human approval gates for sensitive or high-impact actions
- execution logging and auditability for autonomous workflows
- sandboxing or containment for agent actions

### 10. Secure Productivity Workflows

Features:
- governed coding assistant workflows
- secure drafting and review for legal, finance, and compliance content
- internal knowledge Q&A grounded in permitted enterprise sources
- multilingual drafting and translation workflows
- workflow templates for policy, contract, and report generation

### 11. Enterprise Integrations

Features:
- integrations with identity providers (SSO/OIDC/SAML)
- connectors for SIEM, ticketing, endpoint, and collaboration systems
- CASB-style visibility into AI app usage
- integration with enterprise storage, knowledge bases, and repositories
- webhook and event-driven policy enforcement

### 12. Evidence, Reporting, and Assurance

Features:
- standardized evidence bundles for customer and regulator review
- executive dashboards and board reporting
- breach and incident reporting templates
- retention and legal-hold workflows
- exportable governance and compliance records

## V4 Feature Priorities

### Phase A — Trust & Compliance Foundation
- governance workspace
- compliance automation basics
- audit and evidence exports
- third-party risk inventory
- trust center MVP

### Phase B — Workflow & Productivity Expansion
- document intelligence
- meeting and voice protection
- governed agent workflows
- secure knowledge Q&A
- multilingual productivity assistance

### Phase C — Enterprise Platform Scale
- advanced admin portal
- CASB and shadow AI visibility
- deeper SIEM and ticketing integrations
- large-scale governance workflows
- enterprise deployment hardening

## V4 Success Criteria

AnonReq v4 should be able to:
- provide continuous trust and compliance evidence to customers and auditors
- enforce governance across prompts, documents, agents, and workflows
- support secure productivity use cases without exposing raw sensitive data
- integrate into enterprise security and compliance operations
- offer a broader platform experience beyond the core anonymization proxy

## Recommended Scope for V4 Planning

V4 should be treated as a platform expansion, not just another security feature. It should combine:

- secure AI gateway capabilities
- compliance automation and assurance
- governed productivity workflows
- agent and workflow governance
- enterprise trust-center experience
