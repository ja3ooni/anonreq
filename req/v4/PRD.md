# AnonReq v4 PRD

## Product Summary

AnonReq v4 expands the platform from a secure AI gateway into an enterprise AI trust, compliance, and governed productivity suite. The v4 product enables organizations to protect sensitive data while operating AI workflows, demonstrating compliance posture, and enforcing governance across prompts, documents, agents, and tools.

## Problem Statement

Enterprises need more than anonymization. They need continuous trust assurance, governance workflows, compliance evidence, and secure productivity experiences for AI adoption at scale.

## Target Users

- CISOs and security leaders
- compliance and privacy teams
- platform and operations teams
- legal, finance, and risk teams
- enterprise AI platform teams

## Core Goals

- provide enterprise-grade AI governance and evidence
- protect sensitive data across documents, voice, agents, and workflows
- support secure AI productivity without data exposure
- make compliance automation operational and auditable

## Major Feature Areas

1. Governance and risk management
2. Compliance automation and evidence
3. Trust center and customer assurance
4. Secure multimodal and document workflows
5. Agent and tool governance
6. Enterprise integrations and admin controls

## Success Metrics

- adoption by regulated enterprise customers
- reduced time to compliance evidence collection
- improved policy coverage across AI workflows
- lower risk of sensitive data exposure in AI use cases
